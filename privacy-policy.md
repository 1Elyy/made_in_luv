# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## What We Collect

To provide the bot's functionality, we may store:

- Discord Guild IDs
- Discord Channel IDs
- Bot configuration settings (such as welcome messages, sticky messages, auto-react settings, and other server-specific preferences)

## What We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect or permanently store:

- Personal user information
- Direct Messages (DMs)
- Private server messages
- Message content (message content is only processed in real time when required for features such as sticky messages or auto-reactions and is never stored)

## How We Use Data

Collected data is used solely to provide the bot's features and functionality.

We do **not**:

- Sell your data
- Rent your data
- Share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing
- Use your data for machine learning or AI training

Data is collected only to the extent necessary for the bot to function.

## Data Storage

Configuration data is stored securely on a private VPS protected with SSH key authentication and firewall rules.

Data is retained only while the bot remains in your server.

## Data Deletion

When the bot is removed from a server, all associated server data is permanently deleted.

If you would like your server's data removed sooner, you may contact us through our support server.
